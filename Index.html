Frequent Questions

Find out how Blackwell University works right away in case your question or query is frequent.

Who is Blackwell Global University?

Blackwell Global University is an American university located in Orlando, Florida. It offers accredited master's and degree degrees online and on campus.

Is Blackwell Global University licensed?

Yes, Blackwell Global University is licensed by the Florida Commission for Independent Education (CIE), License #3985. CIE is responsible for licensing non-public post-secondary educational institutions in the state of Florida and can be reached at: 325 W Gaines St, Suite 1414 Tallahassee, Florida 32399-0400 | 850-245-3200.(http://www.fldoe.org/policy/cie/)

Where is Blackwell Global University?

University offices are located at: 6100 Lake Ellenor Dr. Patriot Pointe at Orlando Bldg 1. Office hours are Monday through Friday from 9:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m. and 4:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m.

What bachelor's degrees are available at Blackwell Global University?

Blackwell Global University offers two bachelor's degrees:
1. Bachelor of Business Administration
2. Bachelor of Accounting
Both degrees are offered online in English or Spanish, and on campus in English.

What master's degrees are available at Blackwell Global University?

Blackwell Global University offers three graduate degrees:
1. Master of Business Administration
2. Master of Science in Hospitality Management
3. Master of Science in Healthcare Management
Both master's degrees are offered online in English or Spanish, and on campus in English.

How long does an online degree last? Can I do it faster than a traditional career?

Online courses carry the same credit load as on-campus courses, so they require the same amount of time to complete. However, we have flexibility in class schedules, it all depends on how you organize yourself.

Can I work and still do my degree online?

Yes. These titles are designed so that you can study and work at the same time. In fact, 95% of all our online students work and study at the same time.

If I have questions, how can I contact you?

You can contact us by email helpdesk@blackwell.university or through the Blackwell helpdesk. We have instructors, who will gladly support you in your doubts.
